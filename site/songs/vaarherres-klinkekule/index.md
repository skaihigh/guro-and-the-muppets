## About This Arrangement

"Vaarherres Klinkekule" (The Lord's Marble) is a beloved Norwegian song by Erik Bye with music by Finn Luth. Our swing jazz arrangement transforms this classic into a danceable piece perfect for lindy hop.

```
INTRO -> VERS 1 -> VERS 2 -> MELLOMSPILL -> VERS 3 -> VERS 4 ->
[GENE KRUPA BREAK] -> VERS 5 -> OUTRO
```
