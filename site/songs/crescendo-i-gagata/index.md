## About This Arrangement

"Crescendo i gågata" by Lillebjørn Nilsen is transformed into a swing jazz arrangement that cleverly incorporates Beethoven's "Ode to Joy" in the chorus, matching the song's lyrical reference to "Beethoven's Ninth choir tuned up!"

## Key Concept

This song uses **major/minor contrast**:
- **Verse**: G major (bright, happy, optimistic)
- **Chorus**: E minor (dramatic, Beethoven reference)

## Form

```
INTRO (Em) -> VERS 1 (G) -> VERS 2 (G) -> CHORUS 1 (Em - Ode to Joy) ->
VERS 3 (G) -> VERS 4 (G) -> CHORUS 2 (Em) -> BRIDGE (G->Em) ->
[SAX SOLO] -> VERS 5 (G) -> CHORUS 3/TAG (Em) -> OUTRO
```
